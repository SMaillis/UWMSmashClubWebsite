About Me

Hello, my name is Sam, better known in the Wisconsin Smash Scene as TheDood22. I am currently a Computer Science student at UWM and have been a member of this club ever since my first semester at UWM in 2022. I have been playing Smash at least semi-competitively since late 2018 and have been studying Computer Science ever since my Junior year of high school with the goal of being able to transition into the professional world for my field. My main goal with this website is to take two hobbies of mine, Software Development and Super Smash Bros., and combine them into something I can be proud of with this website. Outside of just this website I also want our club and community to grow more because through this club I was able to make a lot of friends at school through our shared hobby of playing Smash and hope I can help others do the same by showing them how much this club has come to mean to us members. With that being said I hope that this website fulfills my vision of it and that it fascinates you enough to join our community, or at the very least enjoy looking through it.

If you have any questions or are looking for more information about our club connect with us via our club discord server. Here we will post scheduling information about our weekly club meetings as well as potentially other Smash Bros. related collegiate events.
