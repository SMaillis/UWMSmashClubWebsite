About The Club

As the name would imply this club is a gathering of Super Smash Bros. Ultimate players with a large emphasis on the competitive aspect of the game. Our club meets every Friday with doors opening and closing between 2:00-10:00 PM while the semester is in session except for special occasions like breaks or finals week. During every meeting we hold a free-to-enter tournament which is scheduled to start at 4:30 PM and consists of a round-robin pools stage (everyone plays everyone in their pool) followed by a double elimination bracket which uses the pools to determine how the bracket is seeded. The idea of this format is so that everyone gets a chance to play a good amount of matches regardless of skill level. This club operates under the assumption of BYOC (Bring Your Own Controller) so if possible bring a controller that can be used on the Nintendo Switch. It is also much appreciated if people can bring their own Nintendo Switch setups to help the tournament run faster as well as have more available setups for games, but this is not required. Lastly, while this club is primarily meant for students, our tournaments are free to enter for anyone who shows up regardless of if you are a student or not. With all of that being said I hope you have a better understanding of what our club is and enjoy looking through the rest of the website.

If you have any questions or are looking for more information about our club connect with us via our club discord server. Here we will post scheduling information about our weekly club meetings as well as potentially other Smash Bros. related collegiate events.
