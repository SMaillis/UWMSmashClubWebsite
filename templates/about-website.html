About This Website

As mentioned in the about me section this website is a personal project made from my passion for both Smash Bros. and Software development. The vision of this website is to serve as an all in one hub for information regarding our club including things like our club schedule, club player power rankings, a tournament bracket archive, ways to get in touch with us, and more general information about our club. My overall goal with this website is to help our club grow bigger and stronger into the future with new students coming in as well as to improve my skills as a Software Developer. Aside from my goals, this website will also serve the purpose of scratching the itch that many stats fanatics (like myself) have of wanting to see the stats of things they enjoy like Smash. With all said and done I hope you enjoy what you see looking through this website and feel inclined to join our community.

If you have any questions or are looking for more information about our club connect with us via our club discord server. Here we will post scheduling information about our weekly club meetings as well as potentially other Smash Bros. related collegiate events.
